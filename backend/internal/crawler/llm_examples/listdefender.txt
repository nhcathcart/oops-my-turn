# ListDefender

> ListDefender is an email list protection platform that automatically cleans email lists, blocks bots and fake signups, and manages subscriber engagement. It integrates directly with CRMs including Keap, HighLevel, and ActiveCampaign to protect sender reputation and improve email deliverability for small businesses.

ListDefender offers three core layers of protection: automated email list cleaning through regular SmartScans, real-time form protection via FormDefender to block bots and invalid submissions, and engagement management to identify and suppress unengaged contacts. Pricing starts at $20/month based on marketable contact count.

The platform performs comprehensive email validation including domain and MX record verification, SMTP verification, disposable address detection, and role-based email identification. All contacts are automatically tagged in the CRM based on validation status (Green - Safe to email, Yellow - Re-engage, Red - Do not email).

## Technical Details

The platform uses SmartScans that perform: domain and MX record verification, SMTP verification, undeliverable address check, disposable address check, vulgar/inappropriate content check, suspicious content check, role-based address check, tag address check, free/business address check, and catch-all domain check. Most validation options are configurable.

FormDefender Enhanced Mode validates that new contacts have been appropriately vetted before receiving emails and can quickly delete contacts that shouldn't have been created, providing an additional layer of protection against sophisticated bots.
